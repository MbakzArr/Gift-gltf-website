The mission

Ride 1783 km to raise funds for 250 bicycles for the youth, to keep them off the streets and grow the talent in our communities.

That line is painted on the back of the support car following Gift down the N1. GLTF has been doing this since 2011, when Gift turned a pile of scrap parts into his first bicycle and then into a cycling career.
